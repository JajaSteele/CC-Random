For easier travel, the following commands
are available for players:
&lime /warp spawn &lightGray : Teleports you to world spawn
&lime /home &lightGray : Teleports you to your home
&lime /back &lightGray : Teleports you to your previous location
(Works for death location too)
&lime /rtp &lightGray : Teleports you to a random place in 
the current dimension
&lime /tpa <player> &lightGray : Teleports you to 
the player's position (once they accept)
&lime /tpahere <player> &lightGray : Teleports the player 
to your position (once they accept)

&white Don't forget to do &lime /sethome &white at your place!
